   - Call `get_better_object` to generate a **lounge set** (sofa, floor lamp, coffee table, round rug, snowman).  
   - Place the lounge set near the **front side of the room**, centered horizontally and slightly closer to the viewer than the room center.

2. **Generate main Christmas tree**  
   - Call `get_better_object` to generate a **main Christmas tree**.
